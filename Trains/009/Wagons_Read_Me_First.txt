These six simple wagons have been designed around the following Peco components.

GR-106 - Archbar wagon bogies.
GR-103 - Coupling pockets (NEM Type).
GR-102 - Couplers (to fit NEM pockets).
GR-105 - Close couplers (hook and eye).

Please note! There is a recess in the underside of the Wagon body to locate the NEM pocket.
